Why this case study exists

Purpose

Translate incident response concepts into a repeatable workflow that a security analyst, business owner, or auditor can follow from first alert through closure.

Scope and assumptions

The organization, account, and event are simulated. The decision logic is modeled on common identity-compromise signals, least-privilege containment, evidence handling, and role-based escalation.

Project outputs

A five-stage response runbook, an evidence checklist, a residual-risk view, and an executive summary that connects technical actions to business exposure.
