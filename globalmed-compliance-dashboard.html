Executive Message

The dashboard converts a long assessment document into a decision-ready risk story.

The strongest exposures come from undefined workflows: access provisioning and recurring UAR, incident escalation procedures, vulnerability scanning cadence, and risk-based patch SLAs. The remediation story is not just "find problems"; it is "sequence the fixes so leadership knows what to fund first."
