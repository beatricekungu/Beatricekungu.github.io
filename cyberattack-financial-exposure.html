Cyberattack Financial Exposure Dashboard

I am developing this as a Power BI project to connect cybersecurity events with the business costs leaders need to understand: downtime, response effort, service disruption, recovery expense, and estimated financial exposure. The finished case study will document the data model, assumptions, DAX measures, and executive dashboard—not present unfinished work as a completed analysis.

Planned project structure

This page will grow as the work is completed. These are the three deliverables I am designing before publishing the final case study.

01 · Data model

Connect events to business services

Structure threat events, impacted services, downtime assumptions, recovery costs, and severity dimensions into an auditable model.

02 · Measures

Calculate exposure transparently

Create documented DAX measures for estimated loss, response cost, exposure by attack type, and changes across time.

03 · Dashboard

Support executive decisions

Design a focused Power BI view that explains material exposure, contributing drivers, and the assumptions behind each estimate.
