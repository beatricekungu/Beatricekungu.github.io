Challenge context

What Maven asked analysts to answer

The Maven Hospital Challenge asks participants to act as an analytics consultant for Massachusetts General Hospital and build a high-level KPI report for executives. I am using that prompt to showcase Power BI storytelling, healthcare operations analysis, financial exposure framing, and governance-minded reporting.

Question 01

How many patients have been admitted or readmitted over time?

Dashboard lens: trend admissions and readmissions by date to reveal patient volume, demand shifts, and care-continuity signals.

Question 02

How long are patients staying in the hospital, on average?

Dashboard lens: calculate average length of stay and highlight where longer encounters may create capacity or staffing pressure.

Question 03

How much is the average cost per visit?

Dashboard lens: summarize visit cost patterns so executives can understand financial exposure by encounter, payer, and procedure.

Question 04

How many procedures are covered by insurance?

Dashboard lens: compare covered and uncovered procedures to show reimbursement risk, payer dependency, and patient cost burden.
