NIST Compliance Audit Tool

This project demonstrates a lightweight cybersecurity compliance review using NIST control concepts. I reviewed sample control evidence, identified gaps, rated risk, and translated findings into practical remediation actions.

Business Problem

Compliance evidence can be difficult to organize, review, and explain clearly across technical and non-technical teams.

What I Built

I created a structured audit review with control notes, gap findings, risk ratings, remediation actions, and source tracking.

Skills Demonstrated

GRC documentation, NIST control review, risk prioritization, evidence evaluation, and cybersecurity communication.
